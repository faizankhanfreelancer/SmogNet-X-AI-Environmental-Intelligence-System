# SmogNet X — AI-Powered Environmental Intelligence & Smart Air Quality Monitoring Platform

## Overview

SmogNet X is a next-generation AI-powered environmental intelligence platform designed to monitor, analyze, predict, and visualize air quality conditions through real-time analytics, machine learning, geospatial intelligence, and interactive dashboards.

The platform was developed to address the growing environmental and public health challenges caused by severe air pollution and smog conditions in Pakistan. By integrating Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Predictive Analytics, and GIS Mapping, SmogNet X transforms complex environmental data into meaningful, actionable insights for smarter environmental decision-making.

Designed with scalability and future expansion in mind, the system demonstrates how intelligent technologies can contribute toward sustainable smart-city ecosystems and data-driven public health awareness.

---

# Problem Statement

Air pollution has become one of the most critical environmental and health-related challenges worldwide, particularly in densely populated urban regions of Pakistan. Major cities frequently experience hazardous smog conditions caused by:

* Vehicular emissions
* Industrial pollution
* Crop residue burning
* Dust storms
* Urban congestion and uncontrolled expansion

Traditional environmental monitoring systems often lack:

* Real-time analytics
* Predictive intelligence
* Automated anomaly detection
* Interactive visualization
* Public health alert systems
* Scalable AI-driven insights

The objective of this project is to develop a complete AI-powered environmental intelligence platform capable of:

1. Detecting pollution anomalies in real time
2. Classifying pollution sources intelligently
3. Predicting future air quality conditions
4. Generating automated public health alerts
5. Visualizing pollution using interactive GIS mapping
6. Delivering dynamic analytics dashboards and environmental insights
